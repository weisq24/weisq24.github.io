About Me

I am currently a lecturer at College of OceanographY and Space Information, China University of Petroleum (East China), specializing in the research of remote sensing image interpretation based on deep learning specializing in the research of remote sensing image interpretation, computer vision, deep learning, and building extraction. Prior to this role, I obtained my PhD and Master's degrees in Photogrammetry and Remote Sensing from Wuhan University under the guidance of Prof. Jianya Gong and Prof. Shunping Ji. Before that, I earned a Bachelor's degree in Geographic Information Science from China University of Petroleum (East China).
